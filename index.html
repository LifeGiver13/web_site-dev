The Moon of Dominion

In the land of Renshō, a vast world under the glow of three moons—Aseru, Yozora, and Shinku—power dictates everything.

The three moons govern the flow of energy in this mystical realm. Their influence imbues individuals with abilities unique to the moon under which they were born. Aseru grants dominion over light and healing, Yozora empowers stealth and shadow manipulation, and Shinku fuels unbridled strength and chaos.


Seikatsu, a seventeen-year-old boy with striking silver hair and mismatched eyes—one blue like Aseru and the other crimson like Shinku—is a rarity. Born under an eclipse where all three moons aligned, he is marked as a Moons-Touched, a being fated for greatness or destruction. Despite his potential, Seikatsu lives in obscurity in the quiet village of Haname, working as a delivery boy for his family’s tea shop.

But Renshō is no peaceful land. The Dominion Wars rage across the continents as moon-blessed factions vie for supremacy. The Lunar Council, an authoritarian group claiming to unify Renshō, sends its elite enforcers to every corner of the land to recruit powerful warriors—willingly or not.

Chapter 1: The Catalyst

One fateful night, under the blood-red glow of Shinku, Seikatsu’s village comes under attack. A faction loyal to the Lunar Council descends upon Haname, seeking to conscript fighters for their war efforts. When his childhood friend, Ayame, is taken for her abilities as a Yozora-blessed shadow-weaver, Seikatsu’s dormant powers awaken. Unleashing a surge of energy that combines light, shadow, and raw destruction, he drives the invaders away but leaves the village in ruins.

Wracked with guilt, Seikatsu decides to leave Haname to rescue Ayame and uncover the truth of his lineage. He carries a single clue: a medallion his father left him, etched with the symbols of all three moons.

Chapter 2: Allies and Adversaries

As Seikatsu ventures into the world, he encounters allies and enemies alike. Among them are:

Kaede: A brash Aseru-blessed healer who dreams of toppling the Lunar Council’s oppressive regime. She sees Seikatsu as both a weapon and a mystery.

Riku: A rogue mercenary born under Yozora, whose moral compass is as gray as the shadows he commands. He initially betrays Seikatsu but later becomes an invaluable ally.

Kagura: A mysterious warrior who wields the chaotic strength of Shinku. She seeks vengeance against the Lunar Council for destroying her clan and recognizes the same destructive potential in Seikatsu.

Together, they navigate a world of treachery, where every encounter tests their resolve. The group uncovers hints of an ancient prophecy foretelling the rise of a Moons-Touched who would either unite Renshō or shatter it beyond repair.

Chapter 3: The Lunar Trials

The journey leads Seikatsu to the Moons’ Crucible, a sacred arena where contenders prove their worth to wield ultimate power. Here, Seikatsu must face challenges designed to test his mastery of each moon’s gifts. Success means gaining the blessing of the moons; failure means death.

Through these trials, Seikatsu’s powers grow—but so does the strain on his mind and body. The alignment of his abilities is unstable, and each surge of power brings him closer to losing control. The weight of expectation and the fear of becoming a harbinger of destruction weigh heavily on him.

Chapter 4: Shadows of Betrayal

As Seikatsu edges closer to the Lunar Council’s stronghold, a devastating betrayal shatters the group. One of his allies reveals themselves to be an agent of the Council, sent to ensure the prophecy ends with Seikatsu’s death. The betrayal forces Seikatsu to confront his deepest fears and doubts about trust and leadership.

Chapter 5: The Eclipse

The story reaches its climax during another rare celestial event—a total eclipse where all three moons align once more. The Lunar Council reveals their true intent: to siphon the power of the moons for themselves, leaving the rest of Renshō powerless. Seikatsu and his remaining allies must stop the ritual while contending with the Council’s elite moon-blessed warriors.

In a final showdown, Seikatsu unleashes the full extent of his powers, transcending the boundaries of the moons’ blessings. He must decide whether to embrace unity or destruction, knowing his choice will shape the future of Renshō forever.
